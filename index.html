About Joseph

Passion, Precision
& Partnership

Joseph Bright is a competitive British Dressage rider and trainer based at his yard in Old Wives Lees, near Chilham and Canterbury in Kent. With over 2,900 British Dressage points and a career spanning from Preliminary through to FEI Grand Prix, Joseph brings deep technical knowledge and a genuine passion for the development of horse and rider.

Known to many as Joe, his approach is sympathetic, progressive and individually tailored — whether working with a nervous novice combination, a horse in need of retraining, or an ambitious amateur aiming for the higher levels. He produces horses from young stock through to international competition level.
